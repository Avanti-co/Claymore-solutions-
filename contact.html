About This Platform

The AVANTI ACCOUNTS MANAGER is a powerful web-based system designed to help businesses manage their inventory, sales, orders, auditing, and financial data all in one place. Built for Claymore Solutions, it provides a sleek, user-friendly interface for capturing stock entries, analyzing income and expenses, viewing audit reports, and maintaining a clear financial overview.

Features include real-time data tracking, automatic calculations of profit margins, smart inventory statistics, and secure storage with Firebase. It's an ideal solution for small to medium businesses seeking reliable digital bookkeeping.

Who Is It For?

- Shop owners who need to keep track of stock and sales daily.
- Accountants who want quick access to audit reports.
- Managers who want to view turnover and profit margins at a glance.
- Any business needing an organized, cloud-based account manager.
